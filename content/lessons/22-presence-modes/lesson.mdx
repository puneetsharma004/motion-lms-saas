When you swap one piece of content for another — a tab panel, a wizard step, a
quote rotator — you usually want the old one to leave *before* the new one
arrives, so they don't overlap or jump. `AnimatePresence mode="wait"` does
exactly that: it holds the incoming child until the outgoing child's `exit`
finishes. The other key detail is a unique `key` per item, so Motion knows the
content actually changed. (`mode="popLayout"` instead pops the leaving item out
of flow so siblings reflow smoothly.)

3. Give the `motion.span` a `key={i}` and `initial` / `animate` / `exit` on `opacity` + `y`.
