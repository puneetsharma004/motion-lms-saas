A good carousel slides the *right way* — new slide comes from the right on Next,
from the left on Prev. The trick is the `custom` prop: pass the direction into
`AnimatePresence` and into each slide's `variants`, and the enter/exit offsets
flip sign based on it. A changing `key` tells `AnimatePresence` the slide
swapped, so the old one exits as the new one enters.

<Instructions>

1. Import `motion` and `AnimatePresence` from `"motion/react"`.
2. Track `[page, direction]` in state; `paginate(dir)` updates both.
3. Wrap the slide in `<AnimatePresence custom={direction}>` and give the `motion.div` a `key={page}` + `custom={direction}`.
4. Define `variants` where `enter`/`exit` use the direction: `(d) => ({ x: d > 0 ? 260 : -260 })`.

</Instructions>

Press Next and Prev — slides enter from the side you'd expect. That directional
awareness is what makes carousels feel right.
