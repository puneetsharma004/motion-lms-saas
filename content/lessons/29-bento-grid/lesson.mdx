"Bento" grids — mixed-size tiles in a tidy grid — are everywhere on modern
landing pages, and the small lift each tile gets on hover is what makes them feel
interactive. It's just `whileHover` with a spring `transition`. No state, no
handlers: Motion tracks hover for you, and the spring gives the lift a bit of
life on the way up and back down.

<Instructions>

1. Import `motion` from `"motion/react"` and make each tile a `motion.div`.
2. Add `whileHover={{ scale: 1.05, y: -4 }}`.
3. Give it a spring `transition={{ type: "spring", stiffness: 300, damping: 20 }}`.

</Instructions>

Hover the tiles — each lifts and springs back. The `col-span-2` tiles keep the
bento rhythm while every cell stays interactive.
